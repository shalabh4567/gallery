About

A Bike, is a human-powered, pedal driven, single-track vehicle, having two wheels attached to a frame, one behind the other. Bike's were first first introduced in the 19 century in Europe and till now more than one billion were in existence at a given time.

Bike invention has had an enormous effect on the society, both in terms of culture and of advancing modern industrial methods.
